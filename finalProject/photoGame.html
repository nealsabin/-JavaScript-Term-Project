Photo Guessing Game

Once complete, the user will be tasked with guessing what an object is in a photo. There will
be a grid of squares overlaid on top of the image. The user will click the squares to reveal
a portion of the photo, with there being only so many reveals available. The user will input
their guess to what the photo is before or once they have reached their guess limit.
